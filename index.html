Welcome

Your Home in Halkidiki

Nestled in the serene village of Kriopigi, our thoughtfully designed two-bedroom apartment blends modern comfort with the timeless charm of Halkidiki. Wake to the gentle rustle of lemon trees in the garden, enjoy your morning coffee on the sun-drenched terrace, and spend your days exploring golden beaches just moments away.

Whether you're seeking a romantic escape, a family retreat, or a quiet place to work remotely, every detail has been considered — so you can simply relax and enjoy.
